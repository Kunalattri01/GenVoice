Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Gen Voice News ("we," "us," or "our") operates genvoicenews.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Site, how we use it, and the choices available to you. By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Information you provide directly — such as your email address when you subscribe to our newsletter, or details you submit through a contact form.
  • Automatically collected information — including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the Site, gathered via cookies and similar technologies.
  • Third-party data — aggregated, non-personally-identifiable analytics from services such as Google Analytics.
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site and its content.
  • To send newsletters and updates to subscribers who have opted in.
  • To analyze usage trends and measure the effectiveness of our content and advertising.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or abuse.
3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors interact with the Site. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent; however, some parts of the Site may not function properly without them.

4. Third-Party Services

We source article content and metadata from third-party news aggregation services (including Event Registry) and may embed third-party advertising, analytics, or social media widgets. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who perform functions on our behalf (such as email delivery or hosting), or when required to do so by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Gen Voice News, our users, or others.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your Rights & Choices
  • You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email we send.
  • Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export the personal data we hold about you.
  • To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.
8. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@genvoicenetwork.com.
